Due to the extensive and indiscriminate use of chemical fertilizers in the last century, the quality of our soil has shown marked deterioration. Compounding this is the shift in the agricultural cropping trend from cereals to cash crops. Soils are indubitably under stress!

Soil conditioners are more than just pH regulators. They are technical products that function in 3 main ways which help to “recondition” soils.

  • Stabilize the soils
  • Correct the pH
  • To make the soils responsive to soil treatments

Soil Conditioners should be selected based on the problem in the soil that needs rectification.

Satrite™

Suitable For Moderately Alkaline Soils

Satrite™ is an inorganic soil conditioner consisting of secondary nutrients like Calcium, Magnesium, Sulphur, Selenium and Silica. It works by removing the excess salts from the soil and hence improving the soil quality while correcting the pH of the rhizosphere.

Epsrite™

Suitable For Acidic Soils

Epsrite™ is an inorganic soil conditioner meant specifically designed to raise pH of the rhizosphere.

Chrocal™

Suitable For Saline/ Sodic Soils

Chrocal™ is formulated specifically for soils that are degraded due to high levels of salt especially Sodium.

Willase™

Willase™ is a reinforced organic product which improves soil texture, increases the water holding capacity and cation-exchange capacity of the soil and decreases the pH of alkaline soils and total sodium content of highly alkaline soils.
