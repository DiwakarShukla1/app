Micmoll™ Series

Micmoll™ Series is a molybdenum-based product used as a preventative measure for a plant to combat diseases.

  • Micmoll-V To combat Leaf Curl and Powdery Mildew issues in Fruity Vegetable
  • Micmoll-G To combat Powdery Mildew issues in Grapes
  • Micmoll-C To combat Citrus Canker

Chalcan 1

Chalcan 1 is a Specialty Neutraceuticals Product for Root-Rot in Vegetable and Cash Crops.

Chalcan 2

Chalcan 2 is a Specialty Neutraceuticals Product for preventing Gummosis in Horticultural Crops.
