Boracol™ Series

The Boracol™ Series, a crop specific formulation of essential micronutrients, is one of our key micronutrient soil application products providing prophylactic benefits to the crop. Boracol™ is developed specifically for plants that cannot be supplied with foliar nutrition like fruit trees and plantation crops. It is a Boron based product with all 6 micronutrients present in plant-available ionic form.

  • Historically the only micronutrient mixture in the micronutrient soil application space since 1976.
  • BALANCED FORMULATION FOR SUSTAINED RELEASE
  • Activity lasts for 75 days post application.
  • No heavy metal contamination.
  • Guaranteed purity of elements!

Crop & Soil Specific Range:

  • Boracol BSF-11: For perennial long duration crops like fruit trees
  • Boracol BSF-12: For annuals/biennials short duration crops- vegetables and for plantation crops.
  • Boracol PERMITE: For short duration crops and cereals
  • Boracol BNJ: Specific Soil Formulation Specifically for Banana
  • Boracol™ GINGER: Specific Soil Formulation Specifically for Root crops like turmeric and ginger.
