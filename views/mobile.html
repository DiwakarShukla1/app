  • S.N.Ranade

    The Institute for Micronutrient Technology (IMT) was started in 1967 by Shrikrishna Ranade who is known as the father of micronutrient technology in India. IMT was started by a technocrat, late, S.N Ranade, who was a Microbiologist of the 1938 vintage. He is rightfully known as the father of Micronutrient technology of India for initiating, introducing and disseminating the concepts of micronutrients in Indian agriculture.

    Our current chairman, the 2nd generation in the business, Dr. S. S Ranade, an MD anesthesiologist, joined the family business in 1973 under the tutelage of S. N Ranade. He has been actively involved in the field of micronutrients and plant nutrition for the last forty years. Under the leadership of Dr S. S Ranade, IMT group has expanded both geographically and operationally. Besides technical and research development, Dr. Ranade has expanded the activity of the organization by setting up high-quality control standards for micronutrient mixture fertilizers and analytical service standards for the agricultural sector. His visionary leadership, broad subject and market knowledge and extensive hands-on experience has taken the company to newer levels. Today the Institute and associate companies are authorities in the field of Integrated Nutrient Management in India.

    With many “firsts” to our credit, 4 manufacturing units in the southern part of India, strong market penetration in the agriculturally important states of India, with over 200 field officers working at the grass-root level, 6 state of the art QC labs & Soil/Tissue analysis labs and excellent brand equity, IMT group is a key player in the Indian micronutrient industry.

    As pioneers of micronutrient technology in India, we have been forerunners in the technical development of multi-nutrient products and custom mixtures to cater to the specific needs of the farmer and the crop. Cutting-edge technology teamed with years of field level experience, has made us leaders in our field both locally and globally. Put together, our group companies have over 150 years of experience in the field of plant nutrition. We are a vertically intergated company with R &D, manufacturing, marketting and exports all under one company banner. IMT is an independent private company with no foreign involvement either financially or intellectually. All intellectual property is wholly created and owned by IMT.

    Ranadey Micronutrients Pvt. Ltd: Manufacturing wing

    We have evolved an array of single and multi-nutrient products that cater to the integrated nutritional requirements of crops. We also manufacture custom mixtures to cater to the specific needs of the farmer. The triage of quality, cost-effectiveness and performance-efficiency of our products gives us our strength. We are one of the few companies that have backward integration of raw-material suppliers as well as forward integration of manufacturing, R &D, QC and marketing all under one banner.

    Our proprietary product profile is extensive and includes -
    • A range of Foliar crop-specific multi-micronutrient products: Boron and Zinc Based (for vegetable crops, cereal crops, horticultural crops and plantation crops)
    • An array of crop & soil specific ground-applied micronutrient mixtures (for banana, ginger, short-duration vegetable crops, horticultural and plantation crops etc.)
    • The first Molybdenum-based crop-specific range of products in India.
    • A range of soil conditioners to rectify acidic, alkaline and sodic soils
    • Fertigation & Drip Irrigation range
    • Biostabilisers to rectify the internal milieu of the cell
    • Spray/Drenching micronutrient formulation to ameliorate “Die-Back” in horticultural crops
    • The first-ever Silica Based Soil Application Gel to ameliorate abiotic stress

    The dictating principles for all our products are crop-specificity, soil-specificity and sound technology. We are committed in developing products of superior quality and value for money for the farmer.

Micnelf™ Series

Micnelf is a boron based multi-micronutrient spray formula which has been used extensively for its prophylactic and a curative benefits. Micnelf is 100% water soluble, easily absorbed by the leaves. Micnelf MS-32 is completely absorbed by the leaf within thirty minutes of application. The action of Micnelf starts within 48 hours and peaks at about 21 days post application.

  • All 6 micronutrients (Fe, Mn, Zn, Cu, B, Mo) incorporated in Micnelf and formulated to work synergistically with ach other.
  • Elements present in plant-available ionic form.
  • BALANCED FORMULATION FOR QUICK RELEASE
  • No heavy metal contamination.
  • Guaranteed purity of elements!

Micnelf crop-specific range includes the following key products:

  • Micnelf MS 16 - Cash Crops-Cotton, Sugarcane
  • Micnelf MS 24 - Fruity Vegetables like Tomatoes, Capsicum
  • Micnelf MS 32- Horticultural Fruit Trees like Grapes, Pomegranate
  • Micnelf MS FLR -Floriculture and Ornamental Flowers

Boracoal™ Series

The Boracol™ Series, a crop specific formulation of essential micronutrients, is one of our key micronutrient soil application products providing prophylactic benefits to the crop. Boracol™ is developed specifically for plants that cannot be supplied with foliar nutrition like fruit trees and plantation crops. It is a Boron based product with all 6 micronutrients present in plant-available ionic form.

  • Historically the only micronutrient mixture in the micronutrient soil application space since 1976.
  • BALANCED FORMULATION FOR SUSTAINED RELEASE
  • Activity lasts for 75 days post application.
  • No heavy metal contamination.
  • Guaranteed purity of elements!

Crop & Soil Specific Range:

  • Boracol BSF-11: For perennial long duration crops like fruit trees
  • Boracol BSF-12: For annuals/biennials short duration crops- vegetables and for plantation crops.
  • Boracol PERMITE: For short duration crops and cereals
  • Boracol BNJ: Specific Soil Formulation Specifically for Banana
  • Boracol™ GINGER: Specific Soil Formulation Specifically for Root crops like turmeric and ginger.

Kiecite™ Series

Fertigation and Drip Irrigation is a fairly new application technique that is gaining acceptance for high value horticulture crops.
