Sassori™

Biostabilizer

Sassori™ is a complex organic metabolic product which enhances the benefits and effects of applied fertilizers and is an excellent support for plants, which tend to revert to the vegetative growth stage. Use of this product stabilizes and reconditions the "internal milieu" of the cell.

Micnics™

Die-back and Root Rot Ameliorator

Micnics™ is an organic based product that is formulated to prevent occurrence of dieback in fruit trees. It is versatile enough to be used for drenching or as a soil application.

Lemspar™

Abiotic Stress Quencher

Lemspar™ is a product specifically formulated to prevent the ill effects of abiotic stress like high temperature, excess rains or freshly reclaimed soils. Lemspar is a Silica based first of its kind - GEL product.
